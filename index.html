Cat Breeds & Traits

From regal show-cats to mischievous alley cats, each cat breed comes with its own special quirks. Here are a few of our ASCII-inspired feline friends:

Siamese

Graceful and chatty, known for their slender frame and striking blue eyes.

Persian

Luxurious fur, a calm demeanor, loves lounging in soft spots all day long.

Maine Coon

Big, fluffy, and sociable. Known as the gentle giants of the cat world.

Calico

A patchwork of colors, fiercely independent yet very affectionate.

British Shorthair

Plush fur, chubby cheeks, and an easygoing nature. Great cuddle buddies.

Sphynx

Hairless and striking. They stay warm by seeking extra blankets or human laps.

Bengal

Wild-looking rosettes and a love for water. Extremely energetic and curious.

Ragdoll

Docile, relaxed, and likely to go limp when held. Often called the puppy-cat.
